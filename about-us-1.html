About Us

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies.By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. Google Developer Student Clubs of LBSITW was initiated by Sruthy S Nair, 2018-2022 ECE, in the year 2021 along with Prof. Nandan S, Assistant Professor ECE Dept., with the aim to create a developer community in the campus for those who are truly inspired by technology. The community has got a bunch of passionate students in its core team who believe in the motto Connect | Learn | Grow.

GDSC LBSITW as a first chapter was inaugurated by Erica Hanson (Global Head of Google Developer Community Programs) and Tanvi Somani (Program Manager at Google) on 20th September 2021.
